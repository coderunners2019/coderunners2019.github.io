THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN LLAMAS AND ALPACAS.


Here are some llama facts:
  • a llama weighs up to 400 lb
  • a llama can grow up to be 46 in.
  • a llama has long and round ears
  • a llama has short hair
  • a llama has a long face
  • a lama has a long neck
  • llamas are mamals
  • llamas are used  for  pulling carts  making wool
  • llamas  are good gard animals and  can carry heavy loads 
  • llamas live in South America
Here are some alpaca facts:
  • alpacas are mamals
  • alpacas can weigh up to 150 lb
  • alpacas live in South America
  • alpacas have pointed ears
  • alpacas are 8 in. shorter than llamas
  • apacas have thick fur
  • alpacas can grow up to be 38 in.
  • alpacas have short faces
  • alpacas are used to make fiber
