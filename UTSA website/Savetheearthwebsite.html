The Best Ways To Save The Earth                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
      Lets start with endangered Animals!
 
  1.     Green Sea Turtle
    • They are endangered because of habitat loss, poaching and entanglement in fishing nets and ingestion of debris in the ocean.
     2.      Red Panda
    • The reason for the red pandas being endangered is because of destruction of their habitat. For example, the trees are being cut down and cleared near their habitat.
     3.       Orangutan
    • The Orungatan is suffering from deforestation in its natural habitat. Its population has dropped byover half in the last 60 years.   
     4.        Malayan Tiger
    • The main reason for them being endangered is because of people illegally hunting them for no reason.
     5.        Sumatran Elephant
    • These elephants suffer from deforestation and habitat loss as a result of human-elephant conflict.
