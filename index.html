Girl CodeRunners 2019


A shortened link to this page: https://tiny.cc/twc2019

A summer camp on basics of coding for middle school girls in grade 6 through 8. A flyer is here

This camp is funded by Texas Workforce Commision through a grant to the University of Texas at San Antonio and runs mainly at Good Samaritan Center

In this camp, the girls will learn about:

Day 1: Coding to create animations and stories using scratch.mit.edu (pdf of instructions)
Day 2: Coding robots using LEGO Mindstroms EV3 (pdf of instructions)
Day 3: Coding to create computer games using scratch.mit.edu (pdf of instructions)
Day 4: Website design using Kompozer (pdf of instructions)
Day 5: Tour of Univerity of Texas at San Antonio and Robotics lab
