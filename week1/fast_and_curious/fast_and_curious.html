UTSA CodeRunners
      CodeRunners is a 5-day camp learning about how to program, code, and create. On the first day, you learn to animate using Scratch, and on the second day, we learned to programEV3 robots. On day 3, we learned to make educational games for little kids using scratch, and on the 4th day, we are learning to make a website. On the 5th day, we will have a field trip to UTSA to learn more about how to code.
